Section 100, Fall 2020

M/W, 11:00 - 12:20, Meen 005

Hyflex Plan: Students assigned into both “A” and “B” groups. Students in the “A” group should plan for face-to-face meetings on Tuesdays, and students in the “B” group should plan for face-to-face meetings on Thursdays. Students who wish to switch from one group to another may do so one time, and must find another student who is willing to switch with them.

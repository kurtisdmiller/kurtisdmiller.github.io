Section Supplemental Information

Section 102, Fall 2020

T/Th, 09:30 - 10:50, Library 117

Hyflex Plan: Students assigned into both "A" and "B" groups. Students in the "A" group should plan for face-to-face meetings on Tuesdays, and students in the "B" group should plan for face-to-face meetings on Thursdays. Students who wish to switch from one group to another may do so one time, and must find another student who is willing to switch with them.

Calendar

All items are turned in on Canvas, and will be considered late after 11:55 pm on the Monday of the following week (US Eastern Time). The calendar for this course is subject to change. Updates will be communicated through the Canvas announcement system. This course will be conducted in four phases: "crawl," "walk," "run," and "fly."
